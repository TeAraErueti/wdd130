New Zealand

New Zealand is a country in the Pacific region of the world with an estimated population of 5.27 million people. New Zealand consists of multiple islands, with the two dominant ones being the North and South Island. Kiwi is the terminology used to describe the iconic bird representing New Zealand as well as the residents of the country. Maori is the native culture to New Zealand with a diverse range of ethnicities that currently reside within New Zealand. The seafood, meat, dairy, farming and timber industry are well known for what New Zealand brings to economic trade. The natural landscapes draw many people to New Zealand each year with breathtaking views that should not be missed.
