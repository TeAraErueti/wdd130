AVAILABLE TRIPS

TRIP DESCRIPTION EQUIPMENT
River Rafting Picturesque scenery of New Zealand's West Coast on calm river waters. This level of rafting still provides a great level of adventure with less rough terrain. We encounter a number of quiet rapids and enjoy a few stops along the river bank where you can stretch your feet and enjoy nature's surrounds. We end our adventure at our lovely local beach where you enjoy a special hot water dip and make your way back to our local eateries for some hearty coasty kai. Life jacket, wetsuit, wool socks, sunscreen, warm hat, sunglasses, water bottle, bag, sneakers, saftey helmet, oar, emergency pack
Rapids Rafting This is our ultimate favorite and popular choice among our customers. This level of rafting provides a mixture of calm to intermediate level rapids. Our guides are fully trained and prepared for even our novice customers to enjoy the thrill of the ride. This full day trip is full of adventure and beautiful scenery with the option to jump out at our half way point to head straight to our secluded end of trip area. We end this adventure at a natural in-ground thermal swimming hole where you will be provided with some of New Zealand's delicious cultural kai and beverage. You have the option to try our lovely kaimoana or hangi platter with vegetarian options available too. Life jacket, wetsuit, wool socks, sunscreen, warm hat, sunglasses, water bottle, bag, sneakers, saftey helmet, oar, emergency pack
Adventure Rafting This adventure is not for the faint-hearted or beginner. For all you adventure junkies out there, this one is for you! Back by popular demand is our adventure experience where we take you through some of the West Coast's roughest terrain. You definitely won't be disappointed! With rough rapids and waterfalls you will be soaked with adrenaline by the end of your trip! Come and take a ride on our adventure bus back to a local resort where you can indulge in a hot spa, enjoy some of New Zealand's finest cuisine and the option to enjoy other local adventures. We have bike riding, rock climbing, hiking, hunting, fishing and many other options available through our partnered resort. Make your way to the oceanside pool or indulge in a massage at the resorts finest day spa. You have the option to stay and make the most of our local hospitality or can book in at any other local available accomodation. Life jacket, wetsuit, wool socks, sunscreen, warm hat, sunglasses, water bottle, bag, sneakers, saftey helmet, oar, emergency pack
Custom Rafting This package is for group bookings only and requires 7 days booking in advance. Tailored packaging is pending on weather, availability of staff and equipment. We understand that our popular packages may not be for everyone so we have provided this option for you. Please feel free to give us a call or better yet, fill in our contact form or send us an email as we would love to discuss how we can best serve you with your adventure needs. Examples of custom packages include: team building, family rafting, birthday package, lake rafting.
