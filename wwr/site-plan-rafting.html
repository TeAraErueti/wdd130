Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to expand into the wider community to build more recognition to potential clients and other adventure businesses willing to collaborate in the future. We are looking at expanding our business by providing corporate and group packages as well as educational and team building options. We are looking for ways to expand to help increase revenue while providing a variety of services to those who appeal to the young adult to middle aged adventure audience. We will look at offering guided trips to multiple locations and further expand into other forms of adventuring through liaising with other adventure businesses. We hope to offer package deals of water rafting with camping, mountain climbing and survival/ scout like camps where people can learn and grow their knowledge base and skillset in outdoor adventures.

Intrepid Adventures began in January 2024 as a reminder and inspiration that despite the worldwide pandemic amongst other challenges, there are still many of us craving that nostalgic thrill of adventure. Come along with us and be inspired to learn from the team as we explore New Zealand's captivating waters hidden among its picturesque nature. We hope you are as excited as we are to make new friends, take some selfies and make wonderful memories within your adventure with us. Lets get adventuring! Lets get wet! Lets get ready to make some awesome memories that will last a liftime!

Audience

Our target audience appeals to the young adult to middle age adventure/ outdoor range. We are targeting people who seek the thrill of adventure and trying something new with outdoor activity.

Who are the target audience? Our targetbase for customers range from young adult to middle age in the middle to upper class financial range. Our services can be costly in this expensive economy so our prices reflect the costs of overhead costs, equipment and maintenance. We will look at seeking community funding and sponsorship to help provide our service to those whose finances may be limited like Make A Wish requests, family service and school requests etc. Those who seek an adventure, outdoor experience, learning experience on the water or trying something new, this is the service for them.

What do they want? Our target audience wants adventure, grounding, learning, a change of scene, a new and exciting experience, a chance to make a great memory by overcoming something in their life through adventure or even team building and growth as an individual and/ or team. We aim to provide an amazing adventure to our target audience by offering package deals, location deals, team building experiences and collaborated experiences in the future with other adventure businesses, such as camping, mountain climbing and biking, and survival/ scout skills.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? We hope our target audience is able to find the right information on our website like who we are, what we offer, contact details, possible experiences with reviews to validate our business and the services we offer, and information on adventure from our links. We aim to make our website mobile, desktop and laptop user friendly.
