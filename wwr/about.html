Intrepid Adventures

Intrepid Adventures began in January 2024 as a reminder and inspiration that despite the worldwide pandemic amongst other challenges, there are still many of us craving that nostalgic thrill of adventure.
Come along with us and be inspired to learn from the team as we explore New Zealand's captivating waters hidden among its picturesque nature.
We hope you are as excited as we are to make new friends, take some selfies and make wonderful memories within your adventure with us.
We aim to provide a variety of adventure to meet your needs here at Intrepid Adventures so lets get adventuring! Lets get wet! Lets get ready to make some awesome memories that will last a lifetime!

History

Intrepid Adventures began in January 2034 with an equipped team of diverse adventure skilled New Zealanders.
The team here at Intrepid Adventures come equipped with a vast range of outdoor and indoor skills to meet your needs.
When the 2019 worldwide pandemic hit, our team collaborated with other adventure savvy groups and people to come up with ways we could still enjoy adventure and deliver our passion to you.

Many zoom calls, planning, trial and error, execution of plans and tweaking has evolved our business into a diverse platform available to you.
We have started with the all time favorite rafting experience as requested from our fans and will continue to branch out our range of services to the public throughout the year.
We hope to see you soon so we can share our passion of adventure with you!
